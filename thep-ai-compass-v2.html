How the two halves fit

The matrix is the stable framework — Layers 1 and 2, reviewed every year or two. Its questions are designed to be asked in SLT meetings, governor sessions and staff CPD; the practical points beneath them are where reflection turns into a decision.

The Radar is Layer 3: the volatile material, watched and summarised so the framework never has to be rewritten just because a new tool or update landed. Every Radar item is tagged against the same six domains as the matrix, so a head can move from "what changed this week" straight to "which of our commitments it touches."
